2024 TOPIK Test Schedule



The Test of Proficiency in Korean (TOPIK) is offered multiple times throughout the year, giving you flexibility to choose a date that works for you. In 2024, there are several opportunities to take the TOPIK depending on whether you prefer the paper-based test (PBT) or the internet-based test (IBT).

Originating in South Korea, TOPIK has gained global recognition as a standardized assessment tool, offering individuals a chance to showcase their Korean language skills.



In 2024, aspiring test-takers eagerly anticipate the TOPIK examination date, a pivotal moment in their language learning journey. While specific dates may vary by region and country, TOPIK typically offers exams multiple times a year to accommodate diverse schedules and preferences. As the world evolves, TOPIK remains steadfast, providing regular opportunities for individuals to demonstrate their linguistic prowess.
